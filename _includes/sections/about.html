About

My name is Jonathan, and I'm from Honolulu, Hawaii. In my free time, I like like listening and trying to make music, longboarding, cooking/baking and making cool stuff. A lot of it starts with the question "Hey, you know what would be cool?" and this website and many of the projects on it are a testament to that.


I currently attend the Olin College of Engineering, a small, project based engineering school just outside of Boston. At Olin, I'm heavily involved in the Formula SAE team where I was the mechanical design lead for three semesters, from June 2018 to December 2019. Each year, we design, build, and race an open wheeled racecar.


As an engineer, I'm particularly interested in applying modeling and simulation to solve complex engineering problems.
